    Discussion

    Discussion based learning is an interactive approach to education that focuses on active student participation through group discussions and debates. This method requires students to express their views, share perspectives and take part in critical thinking instead of following lectures alone. The students learn how to listen, express their opinions and think about the diversity of views, thereby creating an active and constructive learning environment which is more than just lecture based methods.


    Socratic Seminars

    Revisiting the Socratic method of teaching to improve third-year pharmacy students' critical thinking and advanced pharmacy practice experience readiness in a critical care elective.


    Keywords: Critical Thinking, Socratic Method

    The purpose of the study was to enhance pharmacy students' critical thinking, clinical reasoning and APPE training through a Socratic education method in an elective paediatrics course. The results show a significant increase in critical thinking, clinical reasoning and preparedness of students for APPE. In particular, they demonstrated significant improvements in the HSRT reasoning test score, increased positive APPEreadiness survey results, and more favourable teaching style ratings than that of traditional courses. Overall, the Socratic method is highly effective in attracting students and providing significant improvements over a short period of time.
